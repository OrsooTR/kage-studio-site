FAQ

Good to know.

Is it free?

Yes — Kage Studio is completely free.

What do I need?

Windows and After Effects 2022 or newer. The upscale & interpolation tools install themselves from the app.

How do I install it?

Run the installer, open the app, click Install extension, then open it in AE under Window → Extensions.

Does it update itself?

Yes. The app and the panel update automatically — new features arrive without reinstalling.

Will it slow down my PC?

Browsing and editing stay light. AI upscaling uses your GPU only while it’s actually running.

Where do my files go?

Everything runs locally on your machine — imported footage stays on your own disk.
